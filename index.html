About KYTC

The overall goal of the educational programs and other activities covered by the Khan Yunis Training College is to empower Palestinian refugees from the age groups of 14 to 22 years in the Gaza Strip.They are providing the age groups with education, vocational and technical training in order to obtain packages of competitive educational and training skills that help them to contribute in development of the local community in the Gaza Strip and the region.
